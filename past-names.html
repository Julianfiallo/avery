Past Names
An Ode to Avery Clark


This all started in the percussion group chat. Max had just written a cadence for the drumline, and someone jokingly mentioned really long names for songs. Well one was "Max Curtis and the Hunt For Avery Clark: A Perilous Expedition". That name became the song that I wrote for you guys now.
The name took many revisions, from that to "The Hunt for Avery Clark: A Perilous Expedition", to "The Hunt for Avery Clark", then to "A Memoir to Avery Clark", then finally to the name you guys know now. "An Ode to Avery Clark".
